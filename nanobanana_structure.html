Santa Hat

Add a realistic Santa hat to each visible subject in the uploaded image. Automatically detect all subjects with visible heads (including people or animals), and place one Santa hat on each subject's head. Ensure each hat is correctly scaled, positioned, and aligned with the head shape, head angle, and hair or fur line. The Santa hats should appear physically realistic, with natural fabric texture, folds, and shadows that match the original lighting. Strictly preserve the original image: - do not change faces, expressions, fur, hairstyle, pose, body, or clothing - do not modify background, lighting, camera angle, or image style - do not add or remove any other elements Only add Santa hats. No other changes. The final result should look like the subjects were originally photographed wearing Santa hats.
