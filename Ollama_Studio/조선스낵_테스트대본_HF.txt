**Title:** "The Miserly Lord and the Cunning Slave"

**Scene:** A small, run-down courtyard in a noble's residence during the Joseon dynasty.

**Characters:**

* Lord Hwang, a miserly and stingy noble
* Dol-soi, a cunning and clever slave

** Script:**

**Hook:**

( Lord Hwang is seen counting his coins and muttering to himself)

Lord Hwang: (to himself) Ah, I've got to be careful with my money. Can't let anyone take advantage of me. ~구먼, I'm the only one who knows how to manage my wealth.

(Dol-soi enters the courtyard, bowing)

Dol-soi: Lord Hwang, I've brought your lunch. It's a simple meal, but I'm sure you'll enjoy it.

Lord Hwang: (looking at the food) What's this? A small bowl of rice and some scraps of vegetables? You call this a meal? Go back to the kitchen and cook something better!

**Escalation:**

(Dol-soi tries to reason with Lord Hwang, but Lord Hwang becomes more and more agitated)

Dol-soi: But, Lord Hwang, I've already used up all the ingredients we have. If I cook something better, it'll cost more money...

Lord Hwang: (interrupting) Money? You're talking about money? Do you know how much I've spent on you already? You're just a worthless slave, eating away at my wealth!

(Dol-soi looks dejected, but then a sly grin spreads across his face)

**Twist/Punchline:**

Dol-soi: (bowing) Yes, Lord Hwang, I'm just a worthless slave. But I've been thinking... since I'm so worthless, maybe I should just eat all the food myself. (takes a bite of the food) Mmm, it's delicious!

(Lord Hwang's face turns bright red with rage)

Lord Hwang: What?! You scoundrel! How dare you eat my food?!

Dol-soi: (innocently) Oh, I thought you said I could have it, Lord Hwang. ~랑께, you're so generous, after all.

(Lord Hwang's face contorts in anger, and he chases Dol-soi around the courtyard)
