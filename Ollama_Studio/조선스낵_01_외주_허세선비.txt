**Title:** "The Fallen Scholar's Shame"

**Hook:**
In the bustling streets of Hanyang, a self-proclaimed genius scholar, NOH Jung-guk, walks with an air of superiority, his long beard fluttering in the wind. He boasts of his exceptional literary skills to anyone who would listen.

**Escalation:**
As he struts along, he fails to notice a pile of horse manure on the ground. His fancy getup, complete with a hanbok and a traditional hat, makes him an easy target for ridicule. Suddenly, his foot lands squarely on the pile, covering his shoes and hem in a mess of dung and mud.

**Twist/Punchline:**
Just as he's about to clean himself, a group of urchins, led by a mischievous young boy, start pointing and laughing at him. One of them quips, "Hey, look at the 'Gold Scholar' covered in 'Bronze Turd'!" The crowd erupts in laughter, and NOH's face turns bright red with shame.

**Wit and Slapstick:**
As NOH tries to regain his composure, he loses his balance and falls into a nearby mud pit, getting completely covered in the filthy mess. The crowd roars with laughter, and the urchins chant, "Bronze Turd, Bronze Turd!"

**Greed Punished:**
As NOH struggles to get out of the mud pit, he's confronted by the owner of the nearby rice cake stall, who demands compensation for the ruined goods. NOH, still covered in mud, reluctantly hands over his precious silver coins, muttering, "Fine, take it all... I'll just have to live off my exceptional literary skills."

**Final Punchline:**
As NOH slinks away, defeated, the young boy calls out, "Hey, 'Gold Scholar'! You're now the 'Turd Scholar'! Maybe you should rewrite your fate... in the mud!" The crowd bursts into laughter once more, and NOH's face contorts in shame.

THE END. 🎉
